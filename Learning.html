Math: Here is an example of some math in \(\LaTeX\)

The derivative of \(\mathord{\mathrm{f}}(x)\), \(\mathord{\mathrm{f}}'(x)\) is defined by this formula:

\[\mathord{\mathrm{f}}'(x)=\lim_{h\to0} \left(\frac{\mathord{\mathrm{f}}(x+h)-\mathord{\mathrm{f}}(x)}{h}\right)\]

\(\LaTeX\) code:

\mathord{\mathrm{f}}'(x)=\lim_{h\to0} \left(\frac{\mathord{\mathrm{f}}(x+h)-\mathord{\mathrm{f}}(x)}{h}\right)

\(\LaTeX\) is very powerful and versatile, being unbeatable in font quality and formatting. It is mainly used for professional documents by scientists and university students.

Below is some math with no relevance to anything whatsoever, but it is made using \(\LaTeX\).
